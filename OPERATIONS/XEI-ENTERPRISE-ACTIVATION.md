# XEI Enterprise Activation Runbook

Status: **Ready for environment execution**
Family: **XEI-001 through XEI-010**

## Objective

Move the approved XEI family from repository completion into Edward's live executive experience and the first paid Living Company engagement.

## Gate A — Approved deployment

Deploy the repository root to an approved HTTPS host with:

- `index.html` as the XEI-001 entry;
- `series.html` as the XEI-002 through XEI-010 continuation;
- static access to `src/`, `content/`, and release assets;
- no cross-origin restrictions blocking local modules;
- cache behavior that does not strand old runtime files after release.

Record:

- deployment URL;
- deployment commit;
- deployment date;
- operator;
- host configuration;
- rollback target.

## Gate B — Physical acceptance matrix

Execute the full autonomous path on:

1. current iPhone Safari;
2. desktop Safari;
3. desktop Chrome or Edge.

On iPhone additionally verify:

- native keyboard microphone dictation;
- immediate text persistence;
- manual search and chapter navigation;
- automatic proposed-revision scrolling;
- approval control visibility;
- safe-area navigation controls;
- refresh resume;
- background/resume;
- interruption by a real phone call and return.

For every defect record:

- device and operating-system version;
- browser version;
- scene or module;
- reproduction steps;
- expected behavior;
- actual behavior;
- screenshot or recording reference;
- severity;
- corrective commit.

## Gate C — Executive premiere readiness

Before Edward receives the experience:

- clear test session state;
- confirm correct private welcome;
- confirm Marisol and Vianka origin story;
- confirm the embedded BDC publication family;
- confirm manual content, versions, and canonical labels;
- confirm no fake or separate manual preview is used;
- confirm Executive Summary uses real responses;
- confirm company and department selection persist into the series;
- confirm Blueprint Session close is operational.

## Gate D — Edward session

During the self-guided premiere, preserve:

- Living Company assessment responses;
- executive intent;
- live BDC contribution;
- approved manual evolution;
- selected company;
- selected department;
- Blueprint Session confirmation.

Do not replace his real answers with demonstration defaults.

## Gate E — Commercial conversion

Use XEI-002 and XEI-003 to produce:

- Living Company readiness diagnosis;
- enterprise memory-risk map;
- department knowledge architecture;
- 90-day outcome;
- first Living Department scope;
- executive mandate;
- commercial engagement structure;
- expansion path across Checkmate companies.

The first proposal should sell a governed deployment, not generic AI consulting.

## Gate F — First Living Department

XEI-004 begins only after the target company, department, executive sponsor, scope, access, success measures, and commercial authorization are recorded.

Required first-deployment records:

- department charter;
- operating knowledge inventory;
- decision-rights map;
- source-of-truth map;
- Living Manual family;
- training and certification inheritance;
- QA measures;
- executive decision log;
- propagation controls;
- dashboard measures;
- 30/60/90-day review cadence.

## Completion definition

Enterprise activation is complete when:

1. the deployed experience passes the acceptance matrix;
2. Edward completes XEI-001;
3. his real session record is preserved;
4. XEI-002 produces the first real blueprint;
5. XEI-003 produces an approved commercial mandate;
6. XEI-004 begins under governed scope.

Repository completion alone does not satisfy enterprise activation.
