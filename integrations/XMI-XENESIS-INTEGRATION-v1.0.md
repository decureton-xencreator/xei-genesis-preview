# XMI Xenesis Integration Contract

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Active preview integration contract  
**Canonical authority:** `decureton-xencreator/xen-operating-system/architecture/xmi/XMI-001-XEN-MUSIC-INTELLIGENCE-ARCHITECTURE-v1.0.md`

Xenesis may use Xen Music Intelligence as an executive and environmental experience capability for workplaces, hospitality, events, brands, travel, presentations, lounges, showrooms, and customer experiences.

Permitted preview capabilities include:

- context-aware ambient music recommendations;
- brand and venue soundtrack concepts;
- regional and local-scene discovery;
- emerging-artist spotlights;
- event arrival, intermission, networking, focus, and closing soundtracks;
- optional cultural context and curator notes;
- authorized provider links or playlist handoff.

Xenesis must not present unimplemented playback control, provider integration, licensing, or real-time local-scene data as operational. Demonstrations must clearly distinguish live capabilities, prototype behavior, and future architecture.

Location, identity, employee, guest, and audience context must be permissioned and proportionate. Sponsored placement must be labeled. The experience inherits the XMI principle: **Music first. Intelligence second. Education only when invited.**

This repository contains the Xenesis presentation and preview contract only. The canonical XMI architecture remains governed by the Xen Operating System repository.
