# XBM Suite Version 2.0 — Xenesis Integration Contract

## Status
Integration registered. Canonical publication source and release evidence remain in `decureton-xencreator/xen-operating-system`.

## Purpose
Expose the Checkmate BDC Operating System as a governed Living Manual family inside Xenesis without duplicating or weakening its canonical controls.

## Canonical Family
- XBM-101 — BDC Manager Operating System
- XBM-102 — BDC Agent Field Guide
- XBM-103 — Objection Response Fieldbook
- XBM-104 — Script Playbook
- XBM-105 — Objection Handling & Sales Psychology
- XBM-106 — Coaching & Performance
- XBM-107 — Analytics & KPI Intelligence
- XBM-108 — Training & Certification Academy
- XBM-109 — Franchise Deployment & Replication

## Experience Requirements
1. One family landing experience with English and Spanish entry points.
2. Mobile-first responsive navigation and desktop large-monitor presentation.
3. Search by manual, topic, role, service line, objection, KPI, certification, and natural language.
4. Cross-manual links resolve to canonical family ownership rather than duplicated content.
5. Every surface displays publication ID, version, language, canonical status, and last validated build.
6. Print and download actions use certified derivatives only.
7. Preview surfaces must not claim live CRM, training records, analytics, or automation unless connected and validated.
8. External-facing content excludes internal assistant identity and deprecated brand terminology.
9. Mimosa Mi remains excluded.

## Executive Experience
The executive view should surface family health, certification status, bilingual coverage, active modules, release evidence, known gaps, and the next authorized improvement. It must distinguish repository Gold Master from field adoption and business outcome evidence.

## Living Manual Behavior
Approved canonical changes trigger derivative regeneration, family validation, portal refresh, and Xenesis integration refresh. Xenesis never becomes the source of truth for manual text.

## Truth Boundary
This contract registers the experience integration. It does not claim the public preview currently exposes private publication content, live employee data, production CRM, or completed field adoption.
